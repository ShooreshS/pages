iLand (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how iLand handles information when you use the iLand mobile application and related services.

1. Overview

iLand is a digital identity and verified participation platform. The app includes identity onboarding features such as document scanning, MRZ capture, NFC passport reading on supported devices, and related profile and participation features.

Our goal is to minimize data collection and protect sensitive information through privacy-focused design.

2. Information We Process

Depending on the features you use, iLand may process the following categories of information on your device:

  • Identity document information you choose to scan or provide
  • Passport MRZ data
  • NFC passport chip data from supported electronic passports
  • Face image or face-related capture data used during identity-related flows
  • App settings and preferences
  • Identity status and profile-related information generated through your use of the app

3. On-Device Processing and Storage

Core identity data is intended to remain on your device. Sensitive locally stored identity data is encrypted using user-specific keys. On supported Apple devices, hardware-backed security mechanisms may be used, including Secure Enclave where applicable.

We aim to process and store sensitive identity information locally whenever possible.

4. Information We Collect Directly From You

We may collect information that you choose to provide through the app, such as:

  • Identity documents you scan
  • Information extracted from compatible documents
  • Profile or preference information you enter
  • Support requests or communications you send to us

5. Permissions

iLand may request access to the following device capabilities when needed for a feature:

  • Camera for document scanning, MRZ capture, face capture, and related features
  • NFC for reading compatible passport chip data
  • Location for map-based or location-aware features, where available
  • Photo Library only if a feature requires access to images you choose to provide

These permissions are requested in context, when relevant to the feature being used.

6. How We Use Information

We use information processed by the app to:

  • Provide identity onboarding features
  • Scan and review identity information
  • Support verified participation and voting-related features
  • Personalize and improve the user experience
  • Maintain app security, integrity, and reliability
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following circumstances, if applicable:

  • With service providers that help operate or support the app
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or legal process
  • To protect rights, safety, security, or prevent fraud
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets

8. Data Retention

Information stored locally on your device remains there until you delete it, remove the app, clear it through app functionality, or otherwise remove it from your device, subject to platform behavior.

Please note that some secure storage mechanisms on certain platforms may behave differently from ordinary app storage.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information and reduce unauthorized access, disclosure, misuse, or loss.

Security measures may include:

  • On-device encryption
  • User-specific keys
  • Hardware-backed key storage where supported
  • Least-privilege access to device capabilities
  • Secure coding and storage practices

No method of storage, processing, or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children’s Privacy

If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of this policy, please contact us.

  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority
