About iLand

iLand is a privacy-focused platform for secure identity onboarding and verified participation. The app supports document-based identity flows including passport MRZ scanning and NFC passport reading on supported devices.

Key Principles

  • Privacy-focused identity flows
  • Secure on-device storage for sensitive data
  • Verified participation and voting features
  • Clear user control and transparency

Our Mission

iLand exists to help build trusted tools for digital identity, verified participation, and more direct democracy. We believe people should be able to prove they are real, protect their privacy, and take part in important decisions with greater confidence and less manipulation.

Our mission is to create technology that strengthens trust without normalizing unnecessary surveillance. We want to make secure identity and verified participation more accessible, more human, and more useful for real communities.

We believe democracy needs more than ideals. It needs practical, privacy-respecting, secure tools that help people participate credibly, safely, and at scale.
